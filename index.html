About PASS: More Than a Club

The Progressive Academic Student Society (PASS) is a dynamic student-led club thriving within the unique Plymouth-Canton Educational Park (PCEP). PCEP unites Canton, Plymouth, and Salem High Schools, creating a diverse environment of over 6,000 students. PASS is proudly based at Plymouth High School (8400 Beck Rd, Canton, MI), welcoming members from all three schools.

We're not just about grades; we're about building connections. PASS fosters a genuinely supportive, engaging, and fun atmosphere where academic students empower each other. We share learning strategies, tackle challenges together, and celebrate successes as a true community. It's a place to feel homey and connected.

Our Mission

To cultivate a thriving culture of collaborative learning, provide valuable academic resources, and empower every student member to excel through mutual support, shared knowledge, and a strong sense of belonging within the PCEP community.

What We Do: Collaborate & Grow

PASS isn't just talk. We organize engaging activities designed to foster academic success and build lasting connections:

  • Focused Study Groups: Regular, collaborative sessions tackling specific courses (AP, Honors, etc.), challenging subjects, or exam prep (Midterms, Finals, AP Exams).
  • Skill-Building Workshops: Interactive sessions led by members or guests on time management, effective note-taking, research skills, college application tips, presentation practice, and more.
  • Peer Support Network: A core value! Connect with fellow members for help with homework, clarifying concepts, or forming study partnerships.
  • Resource Hub: Members contribute and access a shared pool of helpful notes, study guides, practice problems, and useful academic tools relevant to PCEP courses.
  • Guest Speakers & Panels: Inviting PCEP teachers, counselors, alumni, or local professionals for valuable insights, advice, and Q&A sessions.
  • Community & Fun Events: Relaxed social gatherings, study breaks (with pizza!), team challenges, and activities to help members connect, de-stress, and build friendships in a homey atmosphere.

Helpful Academic Resources

While PASS provides invaluable peer support, don't forget these excellent official PCEP and external resources:

  • Plymouth HS Media Center (Check Canton/Salem sites for theirs) - Databases, research help, books.
  • Canton HS Counseling | Plymouth HS Counseling | Salem HS Counseling - Academic guidance, college planning, support.
  • PCEP Academic Support Programs - District-level resources and initiatives.
  • Khan Academy - Free lessons and practice for Math, Science, AP courses, SAT prep, and more.
  • Quizlet - Excellent for flashcards, study sets, and practice tests for various subjects.
  • College Board AP Students - Official resources, course info, and exam prep for AP classes.

Remember: PASS members often share specific tips, notes, and resources tailored to PCEP classes during meetings and through our communication channels!
