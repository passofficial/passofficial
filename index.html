About PASS

The Progressive Academic Student Society (PASS) is a dynamic school club operating within the vibrant Plymouth-Canton Educational Park (PCEP). PCEP is a unique campus hosting Canton, Plymouth, and Salem High Schools, with a diverse population of over 6,000 students. PASS is located at Plymouth High School (8400 Beck Rd, Canton, MI).

We are more than just a study group; we are a community. We foster a highly supportive, engaging, and fun environment where members empower each other to enhance learning strategies, share knowledge, and confidently achieve their academic aspirations.

Our Mission

To cultivate a thriving culture of collaborative learning, provide valuable academic resources, and empower every student member to excel through mutual support, dedication, and a shared sense of community.

What We Do

PASS organizes a variety of activities designed to support our members' academic journeys and build our community:

  • Focused Study Groups: Regular sessions tackling specific courses, challenging subjects, or exam preparation in a collaborative setting.
  • Skill-Building Workshops: Interactive sessions on time management, effective study techniques, presentation skills, college prep, and more.
  • Peer Support Network: Connecting students for help with homework, understanding concepts, or preparing for tests.
  • Resource Sharing: Members contribute and access helpful notes, study guides, and useful academic tools.
  • Guest Speakers & Events: Inviting teachers, counselors, alumni, or professionals for insights and advice.
  • Community Building Activities: Fun, relaxed events to help members connect and de-stress.

Frequently Asked Questions

Membership is open to all currently enrolled students at Plymouth, Canton, or Salem High Schools (PCEP) who are committed to academic growth and contributing positively to our supportive community.

Currently, there are no mandatory membership fees. We may occasionally ask for small contributions for specific events (like pizza socials), but participation is the main requirement!

General club meetings and specific study group frequencies vary. We typically have larger meetings once or twice a month, with study groups meeting more often, especially around exams. Check our announcements or contact us for the current schedule!

Come to a meeting! You can also follow our Instagram (@passofficialpcep) for updates, or email us. We welcome new members anytime. Leadership opportunities arise periodically.

Ready to Join Our Community?

Elevate your academic journey, make new friends, and become part of a truly supportive group! Joining PASS offers:

  • Access to all PASS study groups, workshops, and fun events.
  • A network of peers ready to help and collaborate.
  • Opportunities to share your knowledge and help others.
  • A chance to develop leadership skills in a friendly environment.

Becoming a member is simple. Attend an upcoming meeting (check announcements!), reach out via email, or follow us on Instagram to get started!
